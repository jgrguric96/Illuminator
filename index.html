Illuminator documentation

The Illuminator is an easy-to-use Energy System Integration Development kit to demystify energy system operation, illustrate challenges that arise due to the energy transition and test state-of-the-art energy management concepts. we utilise Raspberry Pis as the individual components of the energy system emulator, and the simulation engine is based on [Mosaik](https://mosaik.offis.de/).
